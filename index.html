About Me

Innovator.

Hello! I’m Jayant Arora, a 17 year old high school student in the International Business and Technology (IBT) Program at North Park Secondary School. I am also an Innovator at The Knowledge Society (TKS), a program for enthusiastic youth looking to impact the world through the use of emerging technologies. Currently, I'm researching Artificial Intelligence, specifically working with Generative Adversarial Networks. You can see more about this in the portfolio section!

Some of my skills include Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Programming (Python, SQL), Video/Photo Editing (Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, Premiere Pro), Brain-Computer Interfaces and more. You can view my Resume below for more information about my skills, achievements and experiences!
